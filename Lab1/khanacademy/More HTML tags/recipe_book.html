Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Butter, softened 1 cup
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Brown sugar, packed 3/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Eggs 2 large
Semi-sweet chocolate chips 2 cups
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix well.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 300g
Ground beef 500g
Onion, chopped 1 medium
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
Canned tomatoes, crushed 400g
Tomato paste 2 tbsp
Beef broth 1 cup
Red wine (optional) 1/2 cup
Dried basil 1 tsp
Dried oregano 1 tsp
Salt and pepper To taste
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Grated Parmesan cheese For serving
  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, red wine (if using), basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  7. Serve the bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.

Chicken Stir Fry

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast, sliced 500g
Broccoli florets 2 cups
Carrot, sliced 1 large
Bell pepper, sliced 1 large
Onion, sliced 1 medium
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
Ginger, grated 1 tbsp
Soy sauce 3 tbsp
Oyster sauce 2 tbsp
Cornstarch 1 tbsp
Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
Sesame oil 1 tbsp
Salt and pepper To taste
  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  3. Add chicken slices and stir-fry until cooked through; remove from skillet and set aside.
  4. Add sesame oil to the skillet, then add garlic and ginger; stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  5. Add onion, carrot, and bell pepper; stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly tender.
  6. Add broccoli florets and continue stir-frying for another 2 minutes.
  7. Return cooked chicken to the skillet; pour in the sauce mixture.
  8. Stir everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken and vegetables.
  9. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  10. Serve hot over steamed rice.
